SAMSettings.txt
This file sets preferences in SAM at launch.
$SAM_LICENSE 14000
You may disable specific tabs by setting the appropriate value to 0.
$ENABLE_SNAPSHOT 1
$ENABLE_AUDIO 1
$ENABLE_TIMELAPSE 1
$ENABLE_MANAGER 1
$ENABLE_FRAMEEDIT 1
You may also specify a default tab:
Snapshot = 1, Audio = 2, Timelapse = 3,Timeline Manager = 4
$DEFAULT_TAB 1
You may also specify the millisecond wait for movie refresh during playback. 
You may want to increase this for older computers
$MUTE_WHILE_RECORD 0
$COLOR_TABS 0
$APPEND_MODE 1

IMPORTANT: You must restart SAM to apply changes.
